Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to frequently asked questions about our treatments and services

How much does a root canal cost?

It varies depending on the complexity of the problem. It requires an initial check-up to finalise the charge. However, the charge can vary from 3500 to 6000 rupees per session.

Is a root canal permanent?

Mostly yes, they are considered permanent. However, the longevity depends on many factors, including how well the tooth is treated.

Is the root canal procedure painful?

No, because we use modern technology for the procedure with excellent local anaesthesia for comfort.

How long does a root canal procedure take?

Most root canals can be completed within one or two visits. Each session lasts about 60-90 minutes.

Do I need a crown after a root canal?

Mostly yes, because a tooth that has undergone root canal treatment can become more fragile. So, placing a dental crown helps protect it, restore strength, and ensure long-term success.
