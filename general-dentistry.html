Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to frequently asked questions about our treatments and services

How often should I visit the dentist for a general checkup?

We recommend visiting your dentist every 6 months for cleaning and routine checkups.

Is tooth cleaning important?

Yes, professional teeth cleaning is important for long-term oral health. It removes plaque and tartar that brushing and flossing alone cannot. So, we recommend tooth cleaning as the most important step in your oral health.

How can I prevent cavities and tooth decay?

Keeping good oral hygiene is key. Brush twice a day, and floss regularly. Avoid sugary items to prevent cavities. Visit your dentist every six months for a routine checkup.

Are dental X-rays safe? How often should I get them?

Yes, modern digital X-rays use minimal radiation. So, generally, they are safe.

What are the common signs of gum disease?

Usual signs are bleeding gums, bad breath, swollen or tender gums, and receding gums. Watch for these signs and consult an expert dentist.

Is a routine check-up costly?

No. But depending on your needs and issues, the charge may vary.
