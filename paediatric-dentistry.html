Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to frequently asked questions about our treatments and services

When should my child have their first dental visit?

We recommend that their first dental visit be within 1 to 6 months, when the first teeth appear.

How often should children visit the dentist?

Every 6 months for a routine check-up, or more often if your dentist advises.

What causes cavities in children?

Frequent sugary snacks, poor brushing habits, and improper oral hygiene are the main reasons.

How can I prepare my child for their first dental visit?

Keep them positive. Avoid words that scare them.

What should I do if my child has a toothache?

Rinse with warm water, and consult a paediatric dentist if pain continues.
