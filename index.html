Dental Hygiene: The Foundation for Lifetime Wellness

According to the American Academy for Oral & Systemic Health, poor oral hygiene can lead to various health threats. The best dental hygiene routine must start from home, teaching children to brush and floss correctly for their overall wellness. The mouth is a gateway to the body, and keeping it clean helps both adults and children to lead a joyful and healthy life.

Some of the health concerns that arise from poor dental hygiene are:
  • Cardiovascular Disease
  • Alzheimer's Disease
  • Periodontal Disease
  • Diabetes
  • High-Risk Pregnancy
  • Respiratory Issues
  • Rheumatoid Arthritis
