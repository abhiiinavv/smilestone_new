When is oral surgery necessary?

Oral surgery is important for severe tooth decay, jaw alignment issues, dental implants, gum disease, or facial injuries.

Is oral surgery painful?

Modern techniques, local anaesthesia, and sedation make oral surgery safe and comfortable, with minimal pain.

How long does recovery take?

It depends on the procedure. Some surgeries may take weeks to recover from.

What should I eat after oral surgery?

Soft foods, liquids, and cool meals are recommended initially.

Can oral surgery prevent future dental problems?

Yes, oral surgeries can prevent complications and maintain long-term oral health.
